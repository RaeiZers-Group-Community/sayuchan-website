About

RaykujanK13 started Sayuchan to learn coding but ended up turning into something absolutely incredible. He released it to the public, and learned from the steady growth that he was in the right direction and that the public had pretty good taste hehe. And so he continued to rock a service people loved and adored which turned into this bot of awesomeness before you! So far it's been a really fun solo project for Ray and he wishes to share that fun by allowing everyone to invite the bot to your server!
